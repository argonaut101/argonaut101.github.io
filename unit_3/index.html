Cosmic Dreams

We Sell the Stuff of Imagination!

Cosmic Dreams was created in order to bring you the best science fiction the world has to offer. We draw from an international range of authors. Most of our products are in English, either original English-language works or foreign works translated into English. However, we also sell a number of items (about ten percent of our current stock) in foreign languages, primarily French, Spanish, Russian, Japanese, Mandarin and Cantonese.

Our inventory includes:

  • Different categories of science fiction, including:
    • "Hard" science fiction, or technology-based speculative fiction
    • Science fantasy and space opera
    • Apocalyptic and post-apocalyptic stories
    • Cyberpunk
    • Sociological science fiction
  • Printed and audio books
  • DVDs, Blu-Ray and VHS items
