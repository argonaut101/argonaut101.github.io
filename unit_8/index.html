Cosmic Dreams: The Mission

Our aim is to provide the world's best science fiction, drawing from an international range of authors. About ten percent of our current stock is written in a non-English language. We also sell many foreign works in English translations.

Choose from:

  • ”Hard” science fiction, or technology-based speculative fiction
  • Science fantasy and space opera
  • Apocalyptic/post-apocalyptic stories
  • Cyberpunk
  • Sociological science fiction
  • Audio books and videos (numerous formats)

We review, not only books, but also science-fiction-related events in the theatrical and artistic worlds. Reviews are authored by our editorial staff, guest writers, and customers. Our mission: To provide an exceptional “conversation lounge” for our galaxy's inquiring minds!
